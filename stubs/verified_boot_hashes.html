Verified Boot Hashes

If you've locked your bootloader on a verified boot enabled device you can verify that the fingerprint displayed on boot matches as below.
If your device only has a short ID, it is simply the first eight characters of the first line of the SHA-256 hash.
If your fingerprint has colons in it, that is a SHA-1 hash and we haven't figured out exactly how it is calculated.
