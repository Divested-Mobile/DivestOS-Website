About

Our devices have been shipping with more and more proprietary blobs every single year and its just becoming downright stupid. This is merely a patch and the future of our devices is going to end really badly if we keep going in this direction. If we don't get free(er) hardware soon, it'll just result in our personal devices getting more and more locked down with even more blatant malware and then we'll really be stranded.
