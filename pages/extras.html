Firmware Deblobber

This is a simple flashable ZIP used to remove firmware blobs from the /firmware partition. This is necessary in order to remove untrusted proprietary code and to increase security. This for example can prevent exploitation of CVE-2015-6639, an exploit in the Google Widevine DRM TrustZone firmware. WARNING: This permanently deletes these files, the only way to restore them is by flashing your device specific stock firmware image (typically called 'NON-HLOS.img').
