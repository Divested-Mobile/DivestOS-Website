Tweaks

Some tweaks to improve your experience

Settings

  • Configuring 'Profiles' for your uses/environment can increase battery life and enhance security.
  • Disabling lockscreen shortcuts can potentially reduce potential lockscreen bypass vulnerabilities
  • Enable device encryption as early as possible
  • Enable hiding of notification content for enhanced privacy
  • Enable 'Privacy Guard' for extra control over application permissions
  • Enabling 'Increasing ring volume' is very nice if you don't use it already
  • Setting a lockscreen pin of 6+ numbers is extremely recommended

Apps

  • No browser is installed by default, please install 'getChromium' or 'IceCat' from F-Droid. Chromium will be included in future releases.
  • To enable Cell/Wi-Fi location resolving: Install a UnifiedNlp backend from F-Droid and then go to microG Settings > UnifiedNlp Settings > Configure location backends
  • To get Play Store apps, install 'Yalp Store' from F-Droid
  • To pass SafetyNet add 'https://microg.org/fdroid/repo' to your F-Droid repos and install 'MicroG DroidGuard Helper' then enable it under microG Settings > SafetyNet
