Key Features

DivestOS is an unofficial soft fork of LineageOS 14.1. That means you get all the features, device compatibilty, and rapid development just as you would running the official LineageOS.

Increased Privacy

Over 400+ proprietary blobs are removed during build time. Ignoring whether or not they're spying on you, some of these blobs do occasionally send data to third-parties. In addition we also remove LineageOS's built in analytics service (CMStats).

Increased Security

The removal of these proprietary blobs also results in security improvements as they are no longer an attack vector for malicious payloads attemping to escalate their privileges or a remote attacker from sending specially crafted packets intented to target the Wireless Display daemon for example.

Increased Performance

Select devices have their processors overclocked resulting in significant system wide performance improvements. Supported devices also have their system images dexpreopted which results in decreased boot time, especially when full device encryption is enabled.

Increased Battery Life

This one is a bit indirect, but battery life is increased as apps on F-Droid don't have ad/analytics libraries constantly trying to upload your personal information to companies. They also tend to not need to constantly run in the background.

F-Droid Included

F-Droid is an app store that specializes in delivery of FOSS apps. For the most part all apps available through F-Droid respect your privacy and device.

microG Included

microG is a FOSS reimplementation of the proprietary Google Play Services framework that many proprietary apps rely on. In the case that you need to run one of these apps, you can do so without completely exposing yourself to Google. UnifiedNLP, a part of microG, also allows your device to determine its location using Wi-Fi access points and cell towers utilizing offline databases to allow indoor location positioning and battery savings.
